About This Tool

WellPath Analyst

WellPath Analyst reconstructs and compares directional-well trajectories with the Minimum Curvature Method (MCM) — the industry-standard algorithm inside Compass, WellPlan, and StarSteer. MCM joins each pair of survey stations with a smooth circular arc, yielding TVD, N/E, Vertical Section, DLS, and Closure that match commercial survey-management packages to the centimetre.

Built for wellsite geologists, directional drillers, and drilling engineers who need a fast, offline, private way to QC rig-site surveys, compute deviations from plan for morning meetings, analyse formation thickness (MT, AVT, TVT, TST) with true-dip corrections, and export a publication- quality PDF for partners and JV reports. Everything runs in the browser — no cloud, no upload, no leaked well data.

Developed by Ismail Harkat — Senior Wellsite Geologist, Sonatrach (Rhourde Nouss, Algeria) — as an open-source companion to commercial survey-management software. MIT License. Contact: geoharkat@gmail.com.
